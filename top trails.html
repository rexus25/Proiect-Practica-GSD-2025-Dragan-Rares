Top 5 Epic Enduro Trails in Transylvania

Our favourite routes around Sibiu and the mountains nearby

1. Sibiu – Păltiniș Ridge

A mix of forest singletrack, rocky climbs and open views above the treeline. Perfect for intermediate riders who want a bit of everything in one tour.

2. Cindrel Technical Loops

Steeper climbs, rooty sections and switchbacks. Ideal for hard enduro fans who enjoy slow, technical riding more than high speed.

3. Riverbed & Forest Playgrounds

Play zones with log crossings, small step-ups, riverbeds and short loops where we can repeat obstacles. Great for training days and skill progression.

4. Flow Trails Through the Woods

Fast, flowing singletracks in the forest with small jumps and berms. Super fun for intermediate riders and for warming up the first day.

5. Sunset Ridge Tour

An evening ride that takes you on top of the ridges above Sibiu with a sunset view over the mountains. Less technical, more scenic – perfect ending to a riding trip.
