How to Prepare for Your First Enduro Tour

Tips for your first adventure ride in Transylvania

1. Choose the right level

The most important thing is to be honest about your riding level. If it’s your first time off-road, start with a beginner or easy-intermediate tour. You’ll still ride beautiful trails, but without feeling overwhelmed or exhausted.

2. Basics of physical preparation

You don’t need to be a pro athlete, but being in decent shape helps a lot. A few weeks before the tour, try to:

  • Do light cardio (running, cycling, hiking) 2–3 times per week
  • Train legs and core – squats, lunges, planks
  • Stretch – hips, hamstrings, lower back

3. Gear you actually need

At minimum, you should have: helmet, boots, gloves, goggles, knee and elbow protection. A chest protector and neck brace are highly recommended, especially for hard enduro.

4. Hydration and nutrition

Riding enduro is more intense than most people think. Bring:

  • A hydration backpack (1.5–3L of water)
  • Energy bars or snacks
  • Electrolyte tablets if you sweat a lot

5. Mental preparation

The goal is not to “survive” the tour, but to enjoy it. You’ll make mistakes, stall the bike, maybe drop it – it’s normal. Our guides are there to help you, not to judge you.
