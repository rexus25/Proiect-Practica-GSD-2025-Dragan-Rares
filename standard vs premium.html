Standard vs Premium Package – What’s the Difference?

Choose the right package for your riding style and expectations

1. Bike & equipment

In a Standard package, you usually get a very good, reliable enduro bike – perfect for most riders. In a Premium package, you might get the latest model, upgraded suspension, mousses, and extra protection.

2. Riding time and route difficulty

Standard often means a balanced day: nice trails, some challenges, enough breaks. Premium is usually longer riding time, more technical sections and more freedom to push your limits.

3. Support from the guide

Both packages include a professional guide. Premium often means more personalised guidance, smaller groups and more flexibility with routes.

4. Extra services

Premium can include airport transfer, photo & video packages, and extended mechanical support. Standard keeps things simple and more budget-friendly.

5. Which one should you pick?

  • Standard – if you’re budget conscious, first time here, or want a more relaxed day.
  • Premium – if you want the full experience, maximum support, and you ride often.
